About Us

Churn Meter is an innovative web application developed by the students of the University of Engineering & Management (UEM), Kolkata, as part of our 7th semester project. Our mission is to leverage machine learning to help businesses predict customer churn and take proactive measures to enhance customer retention. Churn Meter is designed for versatility, catering to various sectors, including e-commerce, telecommunications, and subscription services. This project exemplifies the innovative spirit of UEM students, combining technical expertise with real-world applications.

With its user-friendly interface, Churn Meter simplifies complex data analytics, providing businesses with actionable insights. Users can easily access key metrics, visualize trends, and receive tailored recommendations to improve customer satisfaction. This predictive capability helps in retaining valuable clients and reduces costs associated with acquiring new customers.

Our Vision

The vision of Churn Meter is to revolutionize customer relationship management by providing businesses with the tools to proactively identify and mitigate customer churn. By leveraging advanced machine learning algorithms and data analytics, Churn Meter aims to create a future where businesses can seamlessly track customer behavior, understand their needs, and enhance overall satisfaction. The ultimate goal is to empower organizations to not only retain customers but also foster loyalty and engagement, leading to sustainable growth.

In daily life, Churn Meter can significantly impact various industries. For instance, in e-commerce, businesses can analyze shopping patterns and identify customers who may abandon their carts. By offering personalized discounts or reminders, companies can re-engage these customers. In telecommunications, providers can identify users who may consider switching services and tailor their offerings to improve customer service.

Overall, Churn Meter transforms data into actionable insights, enabling businesses to build stronger relationships with their customers and create a more satisfying user experience in daily interactions.
